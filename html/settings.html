Features


Interactive Tutorial


If this is your first time using the lvm, then the interactive tutorial is turned on by default. to turn this off, click on the checkmark below.


Show waveforms


By default, waveforms are generated in the video editor.
While useful, the editor freezes while it generates which could be too slow for some with low end computers.
Turning this off will simply add a repeating pre-made pattern in place of true waveforms.


Default watermark


The Video Player has 4 choices for default watermarks.
Default watermarks are shown when you choose a Go!Animate watermark.


14:9 or 16:9 aspect ratio


By default, the Video Player & Editor uses the 14:9 aspect ratio.
However, you can toggle this setting to make it 16:9, just like Vyond's Old Legacy Video Maker.


Appearance


Dark mode


Self-explanatory. Makes the Video List dark.


Decorate your themelist


Self-explanatory. You can customize your themelist. By default, options are set to the gotest334.esy.es/most likely the original GoAnimate (before business friendly) iteration of the themelist.
